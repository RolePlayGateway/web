FAQ

Why Decentraland is important for VR?

For VR to reach its full potential, users need to own the content they build. Blockchain technology is a tool that enables a decentralized digital record of ownership, so it seemed like the perfect tool to create a fair VR world were no-one is in control.

Is Decentraland open source?

Yes, it is. We ❤️ open source.

Does it runs on top of Bitcoin's blockchain?

Decentraland utilizes its own blockchain and network, and thus requires users to run mining nodes.

Who validates transactions?

Miners validates transactions. The network rewards miners by assigning them land. Decentraland uses proof of work, just like Bitcoin.

How land is assigned to miners?

A miner choose which tile is mining. It needs to be a adjacent to existing land.

How often a land is created?

Land is created on every new block. Time between blocks is 10 minutes for testnet. For production it will be ~6 hours.

How can I become a land owner?

You can become a land owner either by having someone transfer you some of their parcels, or by mining new land.

How world content is distributed?

In the Decentraland blockchain, transactions can transfer the ownership of a given land parcel, as well as announce the hash of the scene content of the parcel. The actual content of land parcels is distributed in a P2P manner, via the torrent network.

How can I create content for my land?

You can use Unity3D Editor to create and publish your tile to the Blockchain. This video shows how to do it.

What kind of content I can add to my land?

There are some limitations on what you can do on your tile. For the moment the browser implementation only supports primitive meshes (Cube, Plane, Sphere, Cylinder and Capsule), plain colors and textures.

Does it support Oculus Rift or HTC Vive?

The current version doesn't support it, but we are almost there!

How can I keep in touch?

Please subscribe to our newsletter for important updates and join the conversation on Slack.
